The failing path enters `htmlcut_core::extract::slice::matches::run_validated_slice_extraction`, then `htmlcut_core::document::summary::extract_document_title`, `htmlcut_core::document::parse::select_first`, `scraper::selector::Selector::parse`, `selectors::builder::SelectorBuilder`, and `servo_arc::Arc::from_header_and_iter_alloc`, where Miri reports undefined behavior. The upstream fix belongs in the HTMLCut/FFHN HTML extraction stack rather than in Dataarm.
